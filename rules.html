Design Club Rules

  • 1. Respect the Creative Process
    Appreciate your classmates' work and give constructive feedback. Don't copy others' designs—plagiarism is not tolerated.
  • 2. Come Prepared
    Bring necessary materials (sketchbook, USB drive, headphones, etc.). Have assignments and projects ready on time.
  • 3. Use Equipment Responsibly
    Handle computers, tablets, and software with care. Save your work frequently and back it up regularly.
  • 4. Follow Digital Etiquette
    Use design software and the internet only for class-related work. No gaming, social media, or unrelated browsing during class.
  • 5. Be On Time & Stay Engaged
    Arrive on time and stay focused throughout class. Use class time wisely; stay on task and meet deadlines.
  • 6. Clean Up Your Workspace
    Log off computers and shut them down properly. Return materials and leave your area tidy.
  • 7. Ask Questions & Collaborate
    Don't be afraid to ask for help or share ideas. Support and encourage your classmates' growth.
  • 8. Meet Deadlines
    Late work may affect your grade unless prior arrangements are made. Plan your time—design takes more effort than you think!
  • 9. Follow All School Policies
    Adhere to the school's rules on behavior, internet use, and academic honesty.

Remember: if a student is doing something against the rules or threatening you, don't forget to report them!
